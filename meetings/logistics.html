We will have a block of rooms reserved at the Omni Hotel Montreal. Information on the group rate will follow shortly. Although we have the block available you are by no means required to stay here if you have preferred acoomodations.

Transportation to Omni Hotel from airport:

The Omni hotel is located in downtown Montreal near the McGill University campus.

The Pierre Elliot Trudeau International Airport is an ~30 min drive from downtown.

Taxis are available outside the arrivals area. There is a flat rate between the airport and downtown of $40.

The 747 Bus runs quite often (from once every 10 min to once an hour, depending on the time of day) from the airport to downtown. It is part of Montreal's public transport system, and requires that you buy at least a day pass ($10) on the system (STM). The pass will be good not only for the 747 bus, but all buses and Metro lines for 24h. There is an STM electronic kiosk in the airport to buy passes.

To get to the Omni Hotel, take the 747 bus to Lionel-Groulx metro station and then take the Green Line to Peel station (using same pass as for the 747 bus). The Omni hotel is one block north (i.e. uphill) of Metro Peel on Sherbrooke St. Alternatively the 747 bus can be taken all the way downtown and you can walk or take another bus up to Sherbrooke St., but the metro is likely quicker.

Getting around Montreal:

Public transportation around Montreal is outstanding. There are several Metro (subway) lines downtown and an extensive bus system. 3-day passes are available for $18 and one-week passes are $25.50. One trip tickets are $3.25.

Taxis are plentiful in downtown Montreal and one can usually be hailed within a few minutes.
