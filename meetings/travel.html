The L.A. metro area is well served by numerous airlines operating into multiple airports. Airports to consider are:

  • L.A. International Airport (LAX), a major international airport;
  • Burbank Bob Hope Airport (BUR)
  • Ontario International Airport (ONT)

LAX is more distant from Pasadena and travel costs to Pasadena are likely to be higher, but there are more airlines operating more flights. BUR and ONT are both smaller airports, relatively close to Pasadena, but flight options may be more limited.

Unfortunately, the L.A. metro area is less well served by public transit. Options to travel from an airport to Pasadena include

  • rent an automobile (so that you can feel like a local)
  • taxi
  • Super Shuttle
  • LAX FlyAway Bus (travel from LAX to L.A. Union Station, then transfer to one of the other options or to the Gold Line)
  • LA Metro provides bus and rail service throughout the greater L.A. metro area, including Pasadena. Notable options include the Gold Line from Union Station to Pasadena.

There is no reserved block of rooms for the meeting. Making a hotel reservation soon may be essential as there is likely to be a LIGO Science Consortium meeting overlapping our meeting. It's possible that the LIGO Science Consortium meeting will be quite well attended this year, and hotel rooms may become difficult to obtain in Pasadena.
