Accessing NANOGrav Data

The NANOGrav Data Repository stores all intermediate and final NANOGrav data products. We strive to make data available to collaborators immediately after data processing and verification. Published data sets are also publicly available for download as a tar archive. Scientists that are not members of NANOGrav but who are interested in using proprietary NANOGrav data should contact a senior NANOGrav members to discuss possible collaboration.
